# AI Governance Readiness Checklist

## Leadership and Ownership

- [ ] Executive sponsor identified
- [ ] AI governance owner assigned
- [ ] Security owner assigned
- [ ] Data governance owner assigned
- [ ] Legal/compliance stakeholders identified

## Policy

- [ ] Approved AI use policy exists
- [ ] Prohibited use cases are defined
- [ ] Sensitive data handling rules exist
- [ ] Human review requirements are defined
- [ ] Exception process exists

## Risk Management

- [ ] AI risks are documented
- [ ] Risk scoring method exists
- [ ] High-risk use cases receive review
- [ ] Third-party AI risks are assessed
- [ ] Incident response includes AI scenarios

## Technical Controls

- [ ] Identity and access controls are implemented
- [ ] Retrieval controls are defined
- [ ] API/tool access is governed
- [ ] Logging is enabled
- [ ] Monitoring is assigned

## Compliance and Audit

- [ ] Evidence requirements are defined
- [ ] Audit logs are retained
- [ ] Regulated data flows are documented
- [ ] Reporting process exists
- [ ] Governance reviews are scheduled
