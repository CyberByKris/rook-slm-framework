# ROOK SLM™ Advisory Engagement Outline

## Engagement Purpose

Help the organization evaluate, design, or improve secure and governed AI systems using boundary-first architecture principles.

## Engagement Types

### AI Posture Assessment

Objective:

Evaluate current AI usage, risk exposure, governance maturity, and control gaps.

Deliverables:

- AI use-case inventory
- Risk and control gap summary
- Governance recommendations
- Executive briefing

### Secure AI Architecture Review

Objective:

Review a proposed or existing AI architecture for data, context, tool, model, memory, and governance risks.

Deliverables:

- Architecture review findings
- Boundary map
- Control recommendations
- Implementation roadmap

### Controlled API Orchestration Workshop

Objective:

Help teams design safe patterns for AI tool access and API invocation.

Deliverables:

- Tool risk classification
- API control model
- Approval workflow recommendations
- Logging and audit model

### Executive AI Governance Briefing

Objective:

Equip leadership with a clear understanding of AI risk, governance priorities, and decision points.

Deliverables:

- Board-ready briefing
- AI governance questions
- Risk prioritization matrix
- Recommended oversight model

## Discovery Questions

- What AI tools are currently in use?
- What business processes depend on AI outputs?
- What data is exposed to AI systems?
- What external APIs or tools are connected?
- What logs exist today?
- Who owns AI governance?
- What compliance obligations apply?

## Standard Engagement Flow

1. Discovery
2. Current-state review
3. Risk and boundary analysis
4. Recommendations
5. Executive readout
6. Optional implementation planning
